THIS IS NEW FUTURISM.

This is new futurism, built by those you scorned, built on the foundations of what you wanted to destroy. This is futurism built on reformation, not destruction.

THIS IS FUTURISM THAT WILL HAVE A FUTURE FOR EVERYONE, BUILT BY EVERYONE.

We aim to change current attitudes about the future and technology; we want to encourage the use of technology as a means to build and connect, not to destroy and divide. We want to move forward from the past without feeling the need to demolish it; we intend to use the past as a reference for building the future. We wish to create systems that are flexible; to avoid chaos, time and progress lost during destruction. We want technology, and the future as a whole, to be accessible for everyone; we want to reform outdated methods of communication and sharing.

WE BELIEVE FUTURISM IS ABOUT PROGRESS, PROGRESS THAT CANNOT OCCUR WITHOUT EVERYONE.

This is new futurism, built by your successors. This is futurism, presented on the technology that you praised, technology you don’t understand. This is futurism, your obsolescence.

THIS IS OUR OPPORTUNITY TO CREATE OUR FUTURE.

CREATION

“We will glorify war — the world’s only hygiene — militarism. Patriotism; the destructive gesture of freedom-bringers, beautiful ideas worth dying for, and scorn for woman.”[1]

“We will destroy the museums, libraries, academies of every kind, will fight moralism, feminism, every opportunistic or utilitarian cowardice.” [1]

“With it, today, we establish Futurism, because we want to free this land from its smelly gangrene of professors, archaeologists, ciceroni and antiquarians.” [1]

These are all direct quotes from Filippo Tommaso Marinetti’s The Foundation and Manifesto of Futurism.

WAR, DESTRUCTION, AGGRESSION, CHAOS — THIS IS NOT THE FUTURE THAT WE WANT TO SEE.

We do not need to destroy elements of the past to move forward. Progress is about reformation and change, not destruction. Without the past, there can be no present, and no future at all.

To foster an environment where the past and the future can coexist peacefully in harmony through the use of technology, we will:
Create
systems and technologies that are adaptable and flexible so they can grow and change with humanity.
Build
on previous knowledge and infrastructure to forge new technologies for the future.
Use
technology in a safe and sustainable manner so it connects and builds humanity instead of dividing and destroying.
Preserve
and analyze the past so we can learn from previous errors and improve on past structures.
INCLUSION

Currently only 30% of the STEM workforce is comprised of women

Black women only earn 73% of white men’s salary [3]

The STEM workforce is 89% white and 70% male [4]

This lack of diversity contributes to isolation and a lack of social support in the workplace.

All of this needs to change.
We all have the ability to shape the future, and we are committed to building a future made for everyone. We aim to:
Promote
diversity within the field to break down barriers that prevent minorities from having a voice in planning and decision making
Establish
equal opportunity for everyone regardless of race, sex, age or ethnicity so everyone can equally can participate and contribute to technological advancements and research
Encourage
educational and mentoring opportunities to inspire youths to explore ways they can contribute to the future
Prioritize
the accessibility of technology and resources for individuals from all backgrounds across the world, making technology an open public resource
